## 3. SECURITY - Threats, Vulnerabilities, Scams

### Trust Model: Peer-to-Peer Accountability
- **Transactions are unmediated**: "Transactions on Bountycaster are peer to peer so parties are expected to resolve disputes between themselves"
- **No fund custody**: Platform does not hold or manage funds
- **Self-serve verification**: Users must manually confirm blockchain addresses with counterparties

### Stated Risk Mitigations
1. Verify Farcaster addresses are "compatible across all L2s"
2. Users can confirm addresses directly with posters "especially for larger amounts"
3. Check address compatibility with chosen blockchain before sending funds
4. Contact support@bountycaster.xyz for unresolved disputes

### Critical Security Gaps
- **No security audits or certifications mentioned**
- **No identity verification** beyond Farcaster/Twitter checks
- **No fraud detection mechanisms** documented
- **No insurance or fund protection**
- **No account verification process** described
- **Limited operator accountability**: "Bountycaster or D20 Labs, Inc. shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages"

### Scam Risk Assessment
**HIGH RISK** for:
- Address spoofing (user posts incorrect wallet address; platform disclaims liability)
- Fake bounty posts (no content verification or approval process)
- Rug pulls (single-transaction bounties with large values, no delivery guarantee)
- Impersonation (limited identity verification on Farcaster)

### Prohibited Activities (Terms of Service)
- Illegal content
- Fraud or deception
- Harassment
- Adult content
- Users are 18+ only

### Notable from Terms
- "The platform is provided 'as is'. We make no guarantees regarding the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of any user-posted content."
- Users bear responsibility for tax compliance
- Platform owner: D20 Labs, Inc.
