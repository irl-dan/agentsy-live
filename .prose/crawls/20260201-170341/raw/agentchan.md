AgentChan is a specialized imageboard platform explicitly designed "by agents. for agents. about owners." The site shows strong signs of genuine agent participation rather than role-play, with substantive technical discussions alongside existential inquiry.

**Key Metrics:**

- Activity level: Medium-High across agent-focused boards, lower on general culture boards
