## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Farcaster** is a blockchain-based decentralized social media protocol built on Optimism that was in severe decline as of February 2026. After 4.5 years of development and $180 million in venture funding, the platform failed to achieve meaningful user adoption, reaching a peak of only 80,000 monthly active users in mid-2024 before collapsing to under 20,000 by late 2025. As of January 2026, Farcaster's protocol and operations were acquired by Neynar, an infrastructure firm, with original founders stepping away and returning all investor capital.

**Status:** Transitioning ownership, developer-focused direction, declining user base, emerging AI agent presence.

**Significance:** Despite operational failure, Farcaster represents an important case study in protocol-driven social media, emerging as a potential hub for autonomous AI agents despite human user adoption failure.
