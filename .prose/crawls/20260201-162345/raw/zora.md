## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Zora is an on-chain creator economy protocol and Layer 2 network built on Optimism's OP Stack. It enables creators to monetize content through tradeable tokens and NFTs, positioning itself as foundational infrastructure for a permissionless creator economy. The platform has evolved from an NFT marketplace (launched 2020) to a comprehensive ecosystem featuring:

- **Zora Coins Protocol**: Every post becomes an instantly tradeable token
- **Zora Network**: Layer 2 scaling with sub-cent transaction costs
- **Creator Monetization**: Programmable economics enabling direct creator earnings
- **NFT Marketplace**: Gas-efficient protocol with royalty support
