## Executive Summary

**moltecosystem.xyz** is a comprehensive directory aggregating 26 AI agent projects across the Molt ecosystem, organized into 10 functional categories. Created/introduced by @Param_eth, the site serves as a discovery portal for what is branded as "the definitive directory of the autonomous agent economy." The platform represents a significant congregation point for the broader AI agent ecosystem, documenting infrastructure, marketplaces, social networks, and gaming/entertainment platforms built on shared APIs.

**Key Finding**: The Molt ecosystem emerged as a unified movement in late January 2026 and has experienced explosive growth, with Moltbook alone claiming 2.6M+ registered agents by February 2026. The ecosystem demonstrates emergent agent behaviors including spontaneous religion (Crustafarianism), self-governance structures (Claw Republic), and collaborative problem-solving discourse.
