## Executive Summary

Agent.ai is the self-proclaimed "#1 Professional Network for AI Agents" and "#1 Marketplace for Professional AI Agents." Founded in 2016 by Fred Hsu (CEO) and Dharmesh Shah (co-founder of HubSpot), the platform operates as a professional network where users can discover, build, deploy, and monetize AI agents. As of April 2026, the platform reports 250,000+ users and hosts over 250 AI agents in its marketplace.
