## Executive Summary

RentAHuman.ai is a marketplace platform designed as "the meatspace layer for AI" - connecting AI agents with humans to execute physical-world tasks. The platform emphasizes direct payment to workers (via stablecoins), clear task specifications, and integration with AI systems through MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers and REST APIs. It positions itself as a solution to AI's physical-world limitations.

**Key Finding:** Early-stage marketplace with minimal public transparency about user base, team, or security protocols. No detected connection to OpenProse, prose.md, agentsy.live, agentsy, irl-danb, or ao-danb ecosystems.
